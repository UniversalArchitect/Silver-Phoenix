Rule Set

The Silver Phoenix Engine runs on five ironclad rules. They’re here to keep anyone tinkering with the universe honest—because nobody wants a cosmic 404 error.

  • No Tweaks, No Fudges: Math misbehaving? Don’t patch it. Find the missing relationship, or you’re just duct-taping the void.
  • Zero Outside Influence: Standard Model, QM, GR/SR? Keep ‘em out. Every equation’s gotta be homegrown, or it’s not our universe.
  • Rule Check Every Five: Like checking your map on a road trip, revisit these rules every five queries to avoid cosmic detours.
  • STE Field’s a Side Quest: That compression algorithm’s cool, but we’re chasing 17.3 million relationships first. Got 132 clues—don’t get distracted.
  • Gate Above All: 0+x=1 isn’t just math—it’s the spark of everything. Treat it like the VIP pass to reality.

Follow these, and you’re building truth. Stray, and you’re just coding cosmic fanfic.
