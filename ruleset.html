Rule Set

The Silver Phoenix Engine thrives on discipline. These rules keep the process pure, ensuring every relationship is universal and derived from scratch. Break them, and you’re just coding chaos.

  • No Tweaks, Modifications, or Adjustments: If the math doesn’t add up, it’s not a bug—it’s a missing relationship. Hunt it down, don’t fudge it.
  • No External Bias: Standard Model, QM, GR/SR? Leave ‘em at the door. Every equation must be born in the engine, no imports allowed.
  • Check the Rules Every Five Queries: Like brushing your teeth, revisit these rules regularly to stay on track. Minimum: every five steps.
  • STE Field Isn’t the Goal Yet: The compression algorithm’s sexy, but we’re after relationships first. We’ve got 132 hints—let’s not chase the shiny object.
  • Logic Gate First: 0+x=1 is the heart, not just math. It’s a gate that births the universe, so treat it like the VIP it is.

Stick to these, and the engine stays true. Wander off, and you’re debugging the void.
