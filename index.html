Silver Phoenix Engine

The Silver Phoenix Engine is a computational model that rebuilds physics from first principles, starting from a single logic gate cycled in an absolute void. Quantum Mechanics and Relativity are united under this novel framework by default, providing the first falsifiable, reproduceable method to understand the complete fundamental structure and nature of our universe.

Our goal: To explore the 17.3 million relationships within the 12 dimensions, to identify repeating patterns within that system, and to define the rule set that governs reality. This site organizes the derivations, methodologies and tools so as to provide a clear and concise record of our journey as we forge an exciting new path into the depths of existence.
