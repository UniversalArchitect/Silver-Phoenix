Silver Phoenix Engine

The Silver Phoenix Engine is a computational model that rebuilds the universe from a single logic gate: 0+x=1. Starting from absolute void, it derives 12 dimensions—space, time, energy, and force—through recursive relationships. We’ve built it nine times, verifying a fractal compression algorithm (STE field) that constrains reality’s data. It’s not a game or a sim—it’s a new physics, replacing old models with universal laws.

Our goal: map 17.3 million relationships between dimensional points, verify them without tweaks or bias, and define the universe’s rule set. This site organizes our derivations, methods, and code for clarity and rigor, with tables for the math and guides for the curious.
